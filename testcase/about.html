Welcome to the About page


        We aim to inform the population of the refugee crisis around the world. Our purpose is to utilize data and technology to develop interactive visualizations that will help people comprehend the complexity of the refugee crisis, and to inspire individuals and organizations to take action. Through the use of APIs, we are able to retrieve and analyze data related to refugees, such as population demographics, country of origin, and categories of resettlement submissions. We are working collaboratively to create compelling and interactive visualizations that will effectively communicate the urgency and importance of addressing the global refugee crisis. Our project will provide a user-friendly interface where individuals can easily access and explore data related to refugees. We aim to create a website that is informative, interactive, and accessible to individuals from different backgrounds and levels of knowledge about the refugee crisis. By doing so, we hope to empower individuals to take action and contribute to addressing the refugee crisis in their own way.
